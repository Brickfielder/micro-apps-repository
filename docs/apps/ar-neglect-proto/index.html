📍 Calibration

For accurate assessment, follow these steps:

  1. Place the printed Hiro marker on a flat, well-lit surface
  2. Hold your device 30-40cm (12-16 inches) from the marker
  3. Keep the entire marker visible in the camera view
  4. Maintain a steady position throughout the assessment
