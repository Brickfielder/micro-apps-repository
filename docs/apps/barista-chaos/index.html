Instructions & Commands

Build each order by adding items from Options into your Preparation Tray, then press Serve.

  • Match the ticket chips (size, coffee, milk, sweetener, temperature).
  • Rule — Decaf bell: if the ticket shows Decaf, click 🔔 Ring Bell before serving (ringing with no decaf is neutral).
  • Rule — Extra-hot sleeve: if the ticket shows Extra hot, click 🧻 Add Sleeve before serving.
  • Any-of requirement: when the ticket says add honey or soy, either one satisfies the ticket; both are allowed.
  • Incidents happen: sometimes sugar becomes Honey, or milk must be Soy. The ticket updates accordingly.
